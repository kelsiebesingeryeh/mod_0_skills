class Chair

Attributes:
1. height (integer)
2. has_arm_rests (boolean)
3. material (string)
4. depth (integer)
5. color (string)

Methods:
1. adjust_height (increase or decrease the height of the chair)
2. has_arm_rests (does the chair have arm rests)
3. change_material (changes the material of the chair)
4. add_depth (adds 1 to the depth of the chair)
5. change_color (changes the color of the chair)
