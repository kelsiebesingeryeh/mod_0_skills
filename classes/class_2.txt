class Pizza

Attributes:
1. toppings: (array)
2. crust: (string)
3. sauce: (string)
4. size: (integer)
5. has_baked: (boolean)

Methods:
1. add_toppings (adds another topping to the toppings array)
2. change_crust (changes the crust of the pizza)
3. change_sauce (changes the sauce of the pizza)
4. increase_size (increases the size of the pizza by 6)
5. has_baked (has the pizza been baked)
